Land of the Rivers.

It is home to the world's largest river delta, which is formed by the Brahmaputra and the Ganges river.

Third-largest Military in South Asia.

It maintains the third-largest military in South Asia after India and Pakistan; and has been a major contributor to UN peacekeeping operations.

Hospitality .
